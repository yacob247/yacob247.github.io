Where to Watch the 2026 World Cup

YouTube is an official Preferred Platform partner for the 2026 World Cup. FIFA's own channel carries extended highlights, archive matches and tactical breakdowns, and participating broadcasters can stream the first ten minutes of every match live.

United States FOX and FS1 hold English-language rights; Telemundo and Universo carry Spanish-language coverage.
United Kingdom BBC and ITV split live coverage, with highlights also on BBC iPlayer and ITVX.
Canada TSN and RDS carry the host nation's matches and the wider tournament.
Mexico TUDN and Las Estrellas hold national broadcast rights.

Rights vary by territory — check your local broadcaster for confirmed listings.
