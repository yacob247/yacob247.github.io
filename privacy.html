Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Yacob Digital ("we," "our," "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our company website and its linked product information pages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Google AdSense program policies.

1. Who We Are

Yacob Digital is an independent software development operation. Our company website (this site) introduces our products and provides corporate information. Envizion, our first live product, operates as a separate website under its own domain. This privacy policy applies specifically to the Yacob Digital company website located at envizion.work and its direct subpages (About, Contact, Privacy, Terms, and product information pages).

2. Information We Collect

Our company website is designed to minimize data collection. We do not require account registration, and we do not operate our own analytics tracking. The following information may be collected:

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, your browser automatically transmits certain technical information to our web server and content delivery network (CDN), which may include:

  • IP address and estimated geographic location
  • Browser type, version, and language settings
  • Operating system and device type
  • Referring website URL and pages visited
  • Date and time of each request

This information is used for site operation, security, and performance monitoring. It is not used to build visitor profiles or sold to third parties.

2.2 Information You Voluntarily Provide

If you use our contact form, the form opens a pre-filled email in your own email client addressed to envizionupdates@gmail.com. The information you choose to send (your name, email address, message content) is transmitted directly from your device to our email inbox. We only receive what you voluntarily provide, and we use it solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

This site and its linked products may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies to serve relevant advertisements based on your prior visits to this site and other websites across the internet.

What cookies does AdSense use? AdSense may use cookies for:

  • Personalised advertising: Showing ads tailored to your interests based on your browsing history across sites.
  • Frequency capping: Limiting how many times you see a particular ad.
  • Ad measurement: Tracking ad performance, conversions, and reporting.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity.

Your choices: You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings page. You can also manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or via the cookie consent banner displayed on your first visit.

Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy. We do not control which specific ads are shown or what data Google collects through its ad network.

4. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete any personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt Out: You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information, but if you wish to opt out of personalised advertising, visit Google's Ad Settings.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise your CCPA rights, please email us at envizionupdates@gmail.com with the subject line "CCPA Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

5. Your GDPR Rights (European Visitors)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) grants you the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request restriction of processing your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organisation.
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your personal data, including for direct marketing.

To exercise any GDPR right, email envizionupdates@gmail.com. We will respond within one month. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. Third-Party Services and Their Privacy

Our site loads resources from several third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Fonts — typeface delivery. Google may log font request data.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — CSS framework delivery via CDN.
  • Unpkg CDN — JavaScript library delivery.
  • Unsplash — background image hosting.
  • Google AdSense — advertising, as described in Section 3.

We do not control what data these third parties collect. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for complete information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and products are general-audience resources not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Data Retention and Security

We retain email correspondence (names, email addresses, and messages) for as long as necessary to respond to and resolve inquiries. Server access logs are retained temporarily for security and performance analysis. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, but no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Important: Do not send passwords, payment card numbers, government ID numbers, health information, or other sensitive personal data through email or contact forms. Email is not a secure channel for sensitive information.

9. External Links

Our site links to Envizion (our product), third-party tools, and external resources. Once you follow a link away from this site, the privacy practices of the destination website apply. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be noted on our homepage or via a notification banner. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: envizionupdates@gmail.com
  • Via our Contact Page
