Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Yacob Digital handles information in connection with the company website and its linked product information pages. It is written to be readable — not to obscure what happens with your data. Please read it before using the site or submitting contact enquiries.

1. Who this policy covers

This policy covers the Yacob Digital company website, which includes the homepage, the About, Contact, Privacy, Terms, and product information pages (such as the Envizion page, Creator Suite page, Study Workspace page, and Business Tools page). It does not cover Envizion itself, which is a separate live website hosted at its own domain and may have its own privacy practices.

2. What information is collected

The company website does not require visitors to create an account or log in. No personal information is collected automatically by this website beyond what any standard web server or CDN logs (such as IP address and browser type) may record as part of normal site operation.

If you use the contact form, the form opens a pre-filled email in your own email client addressed to envizionupdates@gmail.com. The information you choose to type into that email (name, message, reply address) is sent directly from your device to that email address. Yacob Digital only receives what you choose to send.

3. Cookies and tracking

The company website itself does not set first-party cookies or run its own analytics scripts. However, it loads resources from third-party services, including:

  • Google Fonts — used to load the Montserrat and Open Sans typefaces. Google may log font request data per its own privacy policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — loads the utility CSS framework. CDN providers may log request metadata.
  • Unpkg CDN — loads the Lucide icon library.
  • Unsplash — some pages display background images hosted on Unsplash's CDN.

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy. Yacob Digital does not control what data, if any, these services collect from visitors.

If display advertising (such as Google AdSense) is enabled on any page, Google and its partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on prior browsing. You can review Google's advertising privacy policy and opt-out options at g.co/adsettings.

4. How contact information is used

Emails sent through the contact form are used only to respond to the enquiry. Contact details are not added to mailing lists, shared with third parties, or used for marketing without explicit consent.

5. Children

The company website is a general information site and is not directed at children under the age of 13. No information is knowingly collected from children.

6. External links

The company website links to Envizion and to product information pages. Once you follow a link away from this site, the privacy practices of the destination apply. Yacob Digital is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites.

7. Data security

Do not send passwords, payment details, government ID numbers, or other sensitive personal information through the contact form. Email is not a secure channel for sensitive data.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to envizionupdates@gmail.com or through the contact page.
