Privacy Policy

How Envizion handles privacy.

This policy explains how Envizion.work treats information when visitors use the site. It was last updated on May 27, 2026.

Local File Tools

Several Envizion tools use browser APIs to process files on your device. When a page says processing is local, selected files are read by your browser for that tool session and are not intentionally uploaded to Envizion servers. You should still avoid using browser tools for highly sensitive files if your device, network, or browser extensions are not trusted.

Basic Site Data

Like most websites, hosting providers and browsers may process technical information such as requested pages, timestamps, browser type, device information, IP address, and referral URLs. This information is used for security, debugging, and understanding whether pages load correctly.

Cookies, Ads, And Third Parties

Envizion may use third-party resources such as fonts, icon libraries, scripts, analytics, or advertising services. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies. If Google ads are enabled, Google may use cookies to serve and measure ads. Visitors can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings and Google ad settings.

External Links

Some pages may link to external websites, documentation, or third-party services. Envizion is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites after you leave this domain.

Contact

For privacy questions or corrections, email envizionupdates@gmail.com or use the contact page at envizion.work/contact.html.
