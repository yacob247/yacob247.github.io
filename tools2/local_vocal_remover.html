How does this work locally?

This tool utilizes your browser's built-in Web Audio API. It applies an advanced Digital Signal Processing technique called Frequency-Filtered Phase Cancellation. Because lead vocals are typically panned to the exact center of a stereo mix, subtracting the Right channel from the Left channel mathematically cancels out the center audio. We apply dynamic high/low-pass filters beforehand to ensure bass lines and kick drums (which are also often centered) are preserved in the final output.
