Engine V8 — VAD-Guided Separation + Spectral Inpainting: A Voice Activity Detection pre-pass now scans the entire track before any FFT work begins. It uses RMS energy, spectral centroid (300–4200Hz vocal register), and zero-crossing rate to score each frame from 0 (pure music) to 1 (singing). The ICC vocal mask is multiplied by this VAD score — so in instrumental sections the mask collapses to zero and the music track is completely untouched. After separation, Spectral Inpainting averages the music spectrum from surrounding clean frames and blends it back into the gaps where vocals were removed, reconstructing continuous texture.
