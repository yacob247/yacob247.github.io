Welcome to Envizion Workbench

Your local engine for life planning, finance, and calculations.

What is Envizion Workbench?

A fully featured, lightweight Excel-like spreadsheet engine. It handles standard formulas like =SUM(A1:B10), alongside 90+ custom life-tools for budgeting, health, GPA, writing metrics, and business planning.

How to use the tools?

Click any cell to type data. Click Add-ons (top right) to open the sidebar. Pick a utility, fill in the inputs, and hit Insert Formula to link it directly to your sheet. Use the ribbon tabs for formatting, charts, data tools, and more.

100% Private & Offline

All calculations and files are processed entirely in your browser. No tracking, no cookies, no data sent to servers. Fully offline-ready.
