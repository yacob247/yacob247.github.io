Welcome to Envizion Workbench

Your localized engine for life planning, finance, and calculations.

What is Envizion Workbench?

This is a fully featured, lightweight **Excel-like Spreadsheet engine**. It handles standard formulas like =SUM(A1:B10), alongside **90+ custom life-tools** for budgeting, health, GPA calculations, writing metrics, and operations planning.

How do I use these Tools?

Click any cell to type data directly. To use our special helpers, click the **Add-ons** button in the top right or go to the **Insert** tab to open the sidebar. Pick a utility, type the inputs, and hit **Insert Formula** to link it directly to your sheet!

Our Open-Book Promise

Your data is **100% yours**. All calculations, files, and formulas are processed completely inside your own web browser. No background tracking, no cookies, and no data gets sent to external servers. It is fully safe, offline-ready, and private.
