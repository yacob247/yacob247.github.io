Welcome to the Ultimate Local PDF Converter & Merger Tool

Managing documents shouldn't require compromising your privacy or paying for expensive software. Our Ultimate Local PDF Converter is designed to offer robust document manipulation completely within your browser. Because the processing happens locally on your device, it can account for an unlimited number of files without server restrictions, upload limits, or security risks.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload Your Files: Drag and drop your files into the designated area or click to select them from your device. You can upload PDFs, Images (JPG, PNG), and Text (TXT) files.
  2. Automatic Conversion: If you upload non-PDF files (like images or text documents), our tool automatically converts them into the PDF format instantly in the background. Unsupported files will safely generate a placeholder PDF to keep your merge sequences intact.
  3. Select Your Documents: Use the checkboxes next to the uploaded items to choose exactly which files you want to include in your final export.
  4. Choose Your Download Method:
    • Merge & Download Selected: Combines all the files you've checked into one single continuous PDF document.
    • Download Separately: Instantly downloads every checked file as an individual PDF document.
    • Randomize & Split: Got 20 files and need them separated into groups? Click this button, choose how many files should go into each group, and the tool will automatically shuffle the files, divide them evenly, merge the chunks, and download them.

Why Choose a 100% Local PDF Tool?

Most online PDF editors require you to upload your sensitive documents to a remote server. This poses significant privacy risks and slows down your workflow depending on your internet connection. Our tool utilizes modern WebAssembly and JavaScript technologies (powered by pdf-lib) to execute complex PDF merging and conversion scripts directly on your computer's RAM.

Key Benefits:

  • Zero Server Uploads: Your private documents never leave your computer.
  • Blazing Fast: No waiting for files to upload or download from a server queue.
  • No File Size Limits: Your only limit is the memory of your own computer. Process dozens or hundreds of files seamlessly.
  • Free Forever: No premium subscriptions, no hidden watermarks, just powerful local processing.
