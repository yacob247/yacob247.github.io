Teleprompter Studio.

A simple local teleprompter for pasted scripts or uploaded text files. Read in a bordered fullscreen pane, track spoken words with blue highlighting, and import voice edits from the separate recorder/editor.

How To Use It

The app runs in your browser. Local system voices are free and unlimited, but the exact voice list depends on the device and browser.

1

Add your script

Paste text directly or upload a readable text file. HTML files are converted to visible text before loading.

2

Read cleanly

Choose a browser voice, reading speed, font size, and light or dark blue mode. The active word highlights in blue.

3

Edit separately

Open the recorder/editor in its own tab for trimming, fades, normalization, compression, DSP vocal tools, phase cancellation, and EQ filtering.
