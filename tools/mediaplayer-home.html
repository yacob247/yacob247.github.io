OmniPlay Studio.

A local media player for reviewing audio and video files with playlist, speed, looping, and lightweight session controls.

Open ToolAll Tools

How To Use It

Useful for reviewing class recordings, draft edits, podcasts, music files, and short video clips.

1

Add media

Select local audio or video files. They stay on the device during playback.

2

Control playback

Use speed, loop, skip, and playlist controls to review the media efficiently.

3

Reset when done

Close the tab or clear the session when the local review is finished.
