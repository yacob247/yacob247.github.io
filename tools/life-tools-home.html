Envizion Excel Workbench.

Envizion Workbench editorial note Envizion Workbench is a practical local tool surface for common planning, writing, creator, finance, and daily decision tasks. This page is published as a utility, but it also needs to explain why the tool exists: the workbench is meant for quick private calculations and structured thinking without forcing a user into an account or upload flow. Some outputs are estimates and should be checked before serious legal, medical, or financial use. The useful value is speed, privacy, and organization: a visitor can open a browser, run a small task, and leave with a clearer next step.

Create a workbook file, use the spreadsheet and add-ons, and let the browser autosave it locally. Workbooks are stored in this browser's local storage on this device.

What Each Area Does

The Workbench combines a spreadsheet grid with small calculators and text/data helpers that can write results into cells.

Planning and health

Dates, goals, sleep, fitness

  • Age, date difference, countdown, sleep-cycle, water, BMI, BMR, protein, pace, and calorie target helpers.
  • Use these for quick estimates and everyday planning, then check important health or financial decisions elsewhere.
Study

Grades, notes, quizzes

  • Build study blocks, timetable chunks, grade averages, final-grade targets, GPA lists, Cornell notes, essay outlines, citations, quizzes, and flashcards.
  • Outputs can be placed into the sheet so notes and calculations stay together.
Money

Budgets and comparisons

  • Use bill split, discount, savings, hourly-to-yearly, loan, subscriptions, unit price, tax, and budget-ratio formulas.
  • These are estimates for planning, not professional finance advice.
Writing and creator

Text cleanup and publishing helpers

  • Count words, estimate reading time, convert case, generate slugs, trim characters, draft headlines, captions, names, thumbnails, and content calendars.
  • Useful when writing and planning content inside the same workbook.
Business and operations

Models, campaigns, capacity

  • Model revenue, break-even, runway, LTV/CAC, campaigns, ROAS, invoices, inventory reorder, capacity, and risk registers.
  • Great for rough planning before a formal spreadsheet or accounting tool.
Data and converters

Lists, JSON, regex, units

  • Remove duplicate lines, sort rows, preview CSV, find/replace, format JSON, extract regex matches, audit data, and convert units.
  • Paste rough text into the sidebar tools or use formulas directly in cells.
