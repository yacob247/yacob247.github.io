AdSense Eligibility & Transparency Protocol

Audited & Compliant

Yacob Digital sites are structurally engineered to fulfill and maintain compliance with the rigorous **Google AdSense Publisher Network Guidelines**. This corporate About platform, alongside our dedicated Legal Frameworks, serves as a validation matrix regarding:

Clear Corporate Identity Transparent ownership with documented parent connections across all product platforms.
No Auto-Scraping / AI Bloat We publish original, helpful text guides conceptualized and hand-coded by human developers.
Logical Navigation Schemes Standardized structures with clear header links, active tabs, and zero hidden redirection code.
Direct Support Lines Explicit email routing systems with direct feedback processes and zero "blackhole" databases.

We ensure that third-party advertising modules (such as Google AdSense) can operate securely across our domains without interrupting the visual aesthetics. Visitors retain complete control over interest-based trackers directly through their native browser cookie control interfaces and global ad personalization managers.
