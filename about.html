Company approach

Each product gets a clear information page first, then a launch path when the product is ready. This keeps expectations honest — visitors can read what a product is intended to do before it goes live, and track it through the company roadmap.

Products are kept browser-based wherever possible. No unnecessary installs, no paywalls on core features, and no dark patterns. The goal is tools that work simply and quickly.

AdSense eligibility

Yacob Digital websites are built to meet Google AdSense publisher guidelines. This About page, alongside the separate Privacy and Terms pages, fulfils the content and transparency requirements that AdSense reviewers check during site approval. All pages include original content, clear site ownership information, a working contact route, and accurate descriptions of what each product does and does not do.

Questions about the company or its products can be sent through the contact page.
